# HERO Wallet — Explainer Intro Video Plan

**Classification**: Internal Production Document
**Date**: 2026-04-28

## Overview

A short, punchy explainer video introducing the HERO Wallet to the community. Uses the HERO soldier logo as the central visual element. Tone: confident, military-inspired, accessible to crypto newcomers.

## Storyboard (8 Clips)

### Clip 1 — HOOK (4 seconds)
**Scene**: Dark screen, HERO soldier logo fades in with a shield glow effect
**Text on screen**: "Your Crypto. Your Keys. Your Mission."
**Narration**: "Marines don't leave their gear unguarded."
**Camera**: Static, center frame
**BGM**: Low military drum roll, building tension

### Clip 2 — THE PROBLEM (6 seconds)
**Scene**: Split screen showing common wallet pain points (complex UIs, hacks in news, confusing gas fees)
**Text on screen**: "Wallets are complicated. Privacy is broken."
**Narration**: "Most wallets leave your transactions exposed. Your balance? Public. Your moves? Tracked."
**Camera**: Pan across problem scenarios
**BGM**: Tension builds, minor key

### Clip 3 — THE SOLUTION (6 seconds)
**Scene**: HERO Wallet UI reveal — clean interface showing Shield/Unshield toggle
**Text on screen**: "HERO Wallet — Privacy by Default"
**Narration**: "HERO Wallet shields your assets with zero-knowledge privacy. One click. Done."
**Camera**: Zoom into wallet interface
**BGM**: Key change to major, energy rising

### Clip 4 — FEATURE: PRIVACY (4 seconds)
**Scene**: Animation showing tokens moving from "Public" to "Shielded" state with ZK proof visualization
**Text on screen**: "Shield & Unshield in Seconds"
**Narration**: "Shield your tokens. Unshield when ready. Nobody sees your hand."
**Camera**: Static with animated elements
**BGM**: Steady rhythm, confidence

### Clip 5 — FEATURE: CROSS-CHAIN (4 seconds)
**Scene**: PulseChain and Base logos connected by a bridge animation, tokens flowing between them
**Text on screen**: "PulseChain + Base + Ethereum"
**Narration**: "Swap across chains. Bridge in seconds. All from one wallet."
**Camera**: Dolly through the bridge visualization
**BGM**: Building energy

### Clip 6 — FEATURE: REWARDS (4 seconds)
**Scene**: XP bar filling up, military rank badges appearing (Private → Corporal → Sergeant)
**Text on screen**: "Earn XP. Rank Up. Get Rewarded."
**Narration**: "Every transaction earns XP. Rank up like a Marine. Get real token rewards."
**Camera**: Static with animated rank progression
**BGM**: Triumphant hit

### Clip 7 — SECURITY (6 seconds)
**Scene**: Guardian Agent visualization — shield icon with scanning animation, threat detected and blocked
**Text on screen**: "Guardian Agent — 24/7 Protection"
**Narration**: "The Guardian Agent watches your six. Anomaly detected? Wallet freezes. You stay safe."
**Camera**: Zoom out to show full security perimeter
**BGM**: Powerful, resolving

### Clip 8 — CTA (8 seconds)
**Scene**: HERO soldier logo center frame, wallet URL below, $HERO and $VETS logos flanking
**Text on screen**: "Join the Beta" / "wallet.herobase.io" / "Built by Veterans. For Everyone."
**Narration**: "Built by veterans. For everyone. Join the HERO Wallet beta today."
**Camera**: Static, hero shot
**BGM**: Final crescendo, military snare roll to finish
